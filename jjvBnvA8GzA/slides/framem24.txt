What are
Smart Cyber Physical Systems?

”The next generation embedded ICT systems that are
interconnected and collaborating, providing citizens and businesses
with a wide range of innovative applications and services” [H2020]

Large—scale software intensive and pervasive systems, combine
various data sources to efficiently control real-world ecosystems

Have to operate under uncertainty

Equipped with intelligence to deal with environment dynamics,
control their emergent behavior, and tolerant to threats

Deliver the ”best possible” service in a given situation.
